Our Story

At BharatPe, the merchants of Bharat are at the core of everything we do.

It’s ingrained in our brand name and built in our DNA.

In 2018, we started our journey to address this gap and aid the growth of merchant partners.

Today, we are a one-stop fintech destination that empowers millions of merchants with easy-to-use, safe and secure financial products.

BharatPe was started in 2018 with the mission to build a holistic FinTech platform for offline merchants and small businesses. Today, BharatPe offers a suite of simple, easy-to-use and secure fintech products including payments and business loans to its merchant partners.
